## Rules

- Complete 2 laps in the shortest possible time using the aira Beta simulator.
- Any control mode is allowed: keyboard, CSV, rule-based, or AI.
- Your **Name** in aira GUI must match your aira account display name exactly.
- Set **Race Flag** to `Submit` and enter `Race_260501` in the Competition field before racing.
- Only your **fastest** result will appear on the leaderboard.
- Invalid results (false start, battery depleted, robot fallen) will not be counted.

## How to Submit

1. Open aira GUI — set **Name** to match your registered display name exactly.
2. Enter `Race_260501` in the **Competition** field (or copy the Race ID from this page).
3. Switch **Race Flag** from `Test Only` to `Submit`.
4. Enter your **Player Token**.  
   Forgot it? Go to your profile page → **Re-issue Player Token** — your token will be resent by email. Your token is permanent and unique to your account.
5. Press **Start**.

Your lap time is automatically recorded on the Leaderboard when the race ends. You may submit as many times as you like during the competition period.
