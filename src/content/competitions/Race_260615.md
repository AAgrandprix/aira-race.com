## Rules

- Run a solo Time Attack to qualify, then compete in a 1v1 algorithm tournament.
- Any control mode is allowed: keyboard, CSV, rule-based, or AI.
- Your **Name** in aira GUI must match your aira account display name exactly.
- Set **Race Flag** to `Submit` and enter `Race_260615` in the Competition field before racing.
- Only your **fastest** Time Attack result is used for seeding.
- Invalid results (false start, battery depleted, robot fallen) will not be counted.

## How to Join

1. Press **Join Competition** on the right side of this page.
2. Open aira GUI — set **Name** to match your registered display name exactly.
3. Enter `Race_260615` in the **Competition** field (or copy the Race ID from this page).
4. Switch **Race Flag** from `Test Only` to `Submit`.
5. Enter your **Player Token**.  
   Forgot it? Go to your profile page → **Re-issue Player Token** — your token will be resent by email. Your token is permanent and unique to your account.
6. Press **Start**.

After the race, your algorithm is submitted as follows:
- **Under 50 MB** — submitted automatically when the race ends.
- **Over 50 MB** — send a download link to [submit@aira-race.com](mailto:submit@aira-race.com).

### Tournament Format

Submitted algorithms compete in a 1v1 bracket streamed on the [aira YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@aira-race).

**Match rules (best of 3)**

- 2 robots compete per match. First to win 2 matches advances.
- Time Attack ranking sets starting positions: faster qualifier = Robot 1, slower = Robot 2.
- Match 1: original positions. Match 2: positions swapped. Match 3 (if needed): same as Match 1.

**Bracket seeding**

| Entries | Bracket |
|---------|---------|
| 2 | Direct match |
| 3 | 1st: bye → final &nbsp;·&nbsp; 2nd vs 3rd |
| 4 | 1st vs 4th &nbsp;·&nbsp; 2nd vs 3rd |
| 5 | 1st: bye → semi &nbsp;·&nbsp; 2nd vs 5th &nbsp;·&nbsp; 3rd vs 4th |

The exact schedule will be announced on the aira YouTube channel before the tournament begins.
