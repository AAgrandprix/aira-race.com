## Rules

- Complete 2 laps in the shortest possible time using the aira Beta simulator.
- Any control mode is allowed: keyboard, CSV, rule-based, or AI.
- Your `NAME` in `config.txt` must match your aira account display name exactly.
- Set `COMPETITION_NAME=Race_260601` in your `config.txt` before racing.
- Set `RACE_FLAG=1` to enable official submission mode.
- Only your **fastest** result will appear on the leaderboard.
- Invalid results (false start, battery depleted, robot fallen) will not be counted.

## How to Submit

1. Set `RACE_FLAG=1` and `COMPETITION_NAME=Race_260601` in your `config.txt`.
2. Complete the race — a **Post Confirmation Panel** appears when the race ends.
3. Review your result and click **POST** to submit to the leaderboard.
4. You may submit as many times as you like during the competition period.
